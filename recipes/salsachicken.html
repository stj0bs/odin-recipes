Salsa Chicken

Crock pot slow roasted salsa chicken

3 ingredient effortless slow roast salsa chicken, place salsa and chicken in and enjoy 10 hours later!

Ingredients:

  • Mateo's Salsa
  • Chicken Breast
  • Chopped Onion

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts into crockpot and turn on "High"
  2. Empty entire jar of salsa into crockpot
  3. Leave for 3-4 hours
  4. Shred chicken in crockpot and stir
  5. Turn crockpot to low and leave for 3-4 more hours
  6. Enjoy!
